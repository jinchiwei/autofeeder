- **Specialist outlets**: publications focused on this exact domain (trade journals, niche blogs, topic-specific newsletters)
- **Analysis/think tanks**: organizations that provide deeper context and interpretation
- **Academic/research**: journals, preprint servers, conference feeds (when relevant)
- **Financial/business press**: for topics with economic dimensions
- **General quality press**: for broader context and public discourse

Before finalizing, verify: Is there any event type from Step 1 with no feed covering it? If so, add a source. Every event type should have at least one feed.
